3-D Design

Using fusion 360 I designed different objects and shapes. I spent the most time creating a phone case made specifically for my phone that has a flip out stand in the back. It tooks lots of measurement and some guessing but the print only midley fit the phone. There were a few adjustments I could of made but, none that are worth reprinting the phone case. We leanred how to 3-d model things and what it takes to make these objects printable.
